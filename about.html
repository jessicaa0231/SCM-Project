About TimeCapsule

What is TimeCapsule?

TimeCapsule is a platform that allows you to create digital time capsules. You can preserve messages, photos, videos, and other files to be opened in the future.

Why TimeCapsule?

Whether it’s a letter to your future self, a memory to share with a friend, or something you want to keep private, TimeCapsule helps you store moments for the future.

How It Works

  1. ✍️ Create your capsule with a message, image, or file.
  2. 📅 Set an unlock date for when you want it to be revealed.
  3. 🔓 Unlock your capsule in the future and relive the moment!

Features

  • 📜 Create and store digital messages, photos, and files.
  • ⏳ Set any unlock date in the future (days, years, etc.)
  • 🔒 Choose to keep your capsule private or share it with others.
  • 📂 Organize capsules by category and folders.
  • 📬 Receive notifications when it’s time to unlock your capsule.
