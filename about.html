About TimeCapsule

What is TimeCapsule?

TimeCapsule is a platform that allows you to create digital time capsules. You can preserve messages, photos, videos, and other files to be opened in the future.Whether it's a letter to your future self, a special memory for a loved one, or a private note to revisit years later, TimeCapsule helps preserve moments so they can be relived exactly when intended.

Why TimeCapsule?

Because some moments are too precious to lose — and too meaningful to share just yet. Whether you're writing a heartfelt letter to your future self, saving a special memory to surprise a loved one, or securing a private message for a later date, TimeCapsule gives you the power to freeze time. It’s not just about storing data — it’s about preserving emotions, stories, and dreams until the perfect moment to relive them. With TimeCapsule, you don’t just remember the past — you re-experience it.

How It Works

  1. ✍️ Create your capsule with a message, image, or file.
  2. 📅 Set an unlock date for when you want it to be revealed.
  3. 🔓 Unlock your capsule in the future and relive the moment!

Features

  • 📜 Create and store digital messages, photos, and files.
  • ⏳ Set any unlock date in the future (days, years, etc.)
  • 🔒 Choose to keep your capsule private or share it with others.
  • 📂 Organize capsules by category and folders.
  • 📬 Receive notifications when it’s time to unlock your capsule.
